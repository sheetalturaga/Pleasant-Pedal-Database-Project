WELCOME TO PLEASANT PEDAL

An app for Seattle-based cyclists, from beginners to experts, to find the optimal times to ride on trails based on expected bike and pedestrian traffic!

FEATURES

Trail Traffic

Provides you with information about any trail in the selected neighborhoods of Seattle and also gives you insights on traffic on given trail at a given time based on the previous data.

Build User Profile

As a user, you can register and create a profile so that you can log in

Review

Once you are logged in, you can review all the trails you have visited that would be displayed on your profile page

Crime Information

Provides you with information on nature & frequency of the crimes committed in the neighborhood of the selected trail
